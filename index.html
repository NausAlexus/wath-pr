Caring for Car Mats

Keep your car mats in top condition with these easy maintenance tips:

Regular Cleaning

Shake off dirt and debris from your mats regularly to prevent accumulation.

Seasonal Washing

Wash mats with warm water and mild soap every three months.

Material Care

Vacuum fabric mats and wipe rubber mats with a soft brush or cloth.
